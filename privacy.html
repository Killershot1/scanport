Privacy Policy — Scanport

Last updated: 2026-02-04

Overview

Scanport is a photo utility app that helps you create and export ID-style photos. This policy explains what data is processed and how it is used.

Photos and Files

  • When you choose a photo (camera or gallery), the image is processed on your device to generate the final output.
  • Exported images may be saved to your device or shared using your device’s share options (e.g., WhatsApp).
  • We do not require you to create an account to use the app.

Advertising (Start.io)

The app displays ads provided by Start.io. Advertising partners may collect device information such as advertising identifiers, IP address, and basic device/app information to deliver and measure ads, and to help prevent fraud.

Permissions

  • Camera: only used when you choose to capture a photo.
  • Photos/Media: only used when you choose to import or save an image.
  • Internet: required for ads and sharing features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with advertising partners (Start.io and their partners) to provide ads and measure performance.

Children

This app is not designed for children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.
